Top Exploits

  • Phishing and Social Engineering

Targets and Bad Actors

  • Ransomware – on Building Automation Servers; This exploit is usually acted upon by foreign Black Hats who are trying to hold critical systems ransom for money. OT is usually the most disruptive to the company without disruption to the customers. It allows for the company to contemplate payment vs. restoration costs
  • Social Engineering – This is used to get customer data or free services by the companies
  • Data Exfiltration – Black Hat actors and often Script Kiddies looking for easy targets to exfiltrate critical PII from customer databases
