There are two main Hospitality Sector being, Accommodation, and Food and Beverage (FnB). Accommodation includes the market segments of Hotels, Motels, Beds and Breakfasts, Resorts, and Cruises. Hospitality is also intertwined with Tourism and Business travel industries. Not to mention many destination resorts and accommodations have other activity-based subsets such as concerts or adventures, such as the Grand Canyon Tour. The other subset of the Hospitality sector is Food and Beverage, these include niche Restaurants, Catering, Bars, Cafes, etc.
